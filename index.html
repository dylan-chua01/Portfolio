About Me

Hey! I am here to share my experiences!

Since young, I have always been passionate about the aviation industry and hope to experience as much as I can, with a dream of becoming a pilot. This motivated me to pursue a degree in Aviation (Management) in the University of New South Wales, Sydney. Since 18 years of age, I have worked in various roles to progress my professional career whilst studying. In July 2023, I was thrilled and over the roof to get my first job with Regional Express. From September 2023, I am now working in one of the worlds leading airlines - Qantas Airways in the Sales & Distribution team alongside my studies. Growing up training swimming 40 hours a week and representing Brunei in multiple international competitions, I have learnt the importance of discipline and hardwork to achieve my career goals.
